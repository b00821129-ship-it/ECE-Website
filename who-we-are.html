Who We Are

At ESSEC Club of Economics, we prepare exceptional students for future key roles in finance and economics through advanced training, mentorship, and industry connections.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: inspire and prepare exceptional ESSEC students for success by providing advanced financial training, industry-focused insights, and exclusive professional opportunities.

We empower our members to excel in competitive global markets and strive to create a meaningful impact in the industries they are passionate about.

Our monthly stock pitches allow our members to enhance their financial and market analysis skills. We aim to create an environment allowing for our members to challenge, guide, and support one another because we believe in the power of collective growth.
