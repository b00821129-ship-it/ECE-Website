Our Approach

At ESSEC Club of Economics, we aim to prepare star students for future key roles in various industries.

Our monthly stock pitches allow our members to enhance their financial and market analysis skills. We aim to create an environment allowing for our members to challenge, guide, and support one another because we believe in the power of collective growth.

Our events, courses, partners, and mentorship programs help students become impactful future professionals in the industries they are passionate about.

ECE Singapore Branch:

At ESSEC Club of Economics, we bridge our European roots with the dynamic markets of the Asia-Pacific through our Singapore branch. This cross-continental synergy fosters a seamless exchange of global perspectives and localized expertise, from established Western markets to high-growth Asian economies. By linking our members across continents, we provide a truly international network and the cultural agility required to lead in the world’s most competitive financial centers, reinforcing our position as a global leader in student-led financial excellence.
